Trust & Safety
Compliance built in from day one
🔐
GDPR / Article 9
Special category data (health certs, ID) handled under explicit consent with DPA agreements. Right to erasure, breach notification within 72h.
🏥
BASHH Integration
Health certificate verification via BASHH clinic network. Verified certs displayed on profile with expiry — no cert, no booking.
✅
ID Verified Badge
Government ID + social profile cross-check on every account. Verified badge visible across all discovery surfaces.
📋
ICO Registration
CollabSpace registered with the Information Commissioner's Office as a data controller processing special category data.
⚖️
Contract Protection
Every shoot covered by a platform-generated contract. Built-in IP assignment, model release, payment terms, and cancellation clauses.

Data Retention
Health data retained for 90 days max. ID data encrypted at rest, hashed post-verification. Full audit trail per regulatory requirement.
